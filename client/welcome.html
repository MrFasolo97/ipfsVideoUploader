A new method of posting to DTube is born. Upload your cutting edge videos onto our high performance, decentralized IPFS nodes. Your videos will remain on the IPFS network indefinitely.
Stuck in upload queue? You can skip it by using our upload servers instead! IPFS operations are handled asynchronously, means there's no need to wait in a queue to add your outstanding video to the network!
Hate entering private keys on websites? Our uploader supports Hive Keychain and HiveConnect, so your Hive transactions are signed securely without exposing your Hive keys to anyone!
DTube servers down? Videos unplayable on DTube? Our uploader uploads your video straight to the public IPFS network, without involving DTube's upload servers. Your video will remain playable even during DTube's downtime, as long as it is available in the IPFS network!
